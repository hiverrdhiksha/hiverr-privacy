To personalise your brushing plan and save your streak and session history across devices.

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at hiverr.dhiksha@gmail.com. Local device data can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

All data transmitted by hiverr. is encrypted using HTTPS. We do not sell your personal data to any third parties.

Your Rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access, correct or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Children

hiverr. is suitable for users aged 6 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.
